The Brix Standard

Quality isn't accidental. It's the result of rigorous standards at every stage of processing at our wet mill.

View Processing Details arrow_forward

01 Selective Picking

Our harvesters are trained to pick only the deepest red cherries, ensuring maximum sugar content (Brix) and flavor potential.

02 Controlled Fermentation

We use state-of-the-art tanks to control temperature and PH levels, developing unique acidity and complex body in every lot.

03 Precision Drying

Our coffee dries slowly on raised African beds, protected from the elements to ensure stability and a long shelf life.
